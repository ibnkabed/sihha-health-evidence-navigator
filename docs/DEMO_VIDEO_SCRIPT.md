# Public demo video script — target 2:35

## 0:00–0:18 — Problem

**Voiceover:** “A smartwatch can collect thousands of health measurements, but most people still cannot explain what changed or what to ask at their next appointment. Sihha turns scattered evidence into a private, understandable conversation brief.”

Show the Overview page and the Safe demo badge.

## 0:18–0:48 — Apple Health ZIP

**Voiceover:** “On iPhone, Health can export a compressed `export.zip`. Sihha opens that ZIP inside the browser, reads `export.xml`, and extracts steps, sleep, resting heart rate, exercise minutes, and workouts. The file is never uploaded.”

Show the **استيراد Apple Health ZIP** button, then open Activity using the synthetic demo.

## 0:48–1:20 — Smartwatch activity

**Voiceover:** “The Activity page replaces disconnected numbers with a seven-day pattern. It adds a non-medical Today score, shows what changed, and states how complete the evidence is. Making progress visible gives people a reason to keep moving, exercise more consistently, and pay closer attention to their health — without claiming causation or medical readiness.”

Show steps, sleep bars, and pattern card.

## 1:20–1:48 — Labs and supplements

**Voiceover:** “Lab results are shown as trends, not isolated red flags. Each result keeps the reference, latest value, direction, and a suggested question for a clinician. The supplement view organizes what the user already recorded; it never recommends starting or stopping anything.”

Show LDL, then the supplement table.

## 1:48–2:12 — Brief and privacy

**Voiceover:** “Sihha creates an Arabic or English evidence brief that the user can print, copy, or download. Nothing is shared automatically. The demo is synthetic, and real imports stay in browser memory.”

Show English report and Privacy page.
